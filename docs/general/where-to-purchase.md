# Where to Purchase

Inferno Collection resources can be purchased from our store:

> [https://store.inferno-collection.com/](https://store.inferno-collection.com/)

After purchasing a resource, download it from the [CFX Portal](https://portal.cfx.re/assets/granted-assets?search=Inferno+Collection) using the same Cfx.re account you used at checkout.

:::tip
If you are looking for Sonoran Edition resources or add-ons, these are also available from the store.
:::
